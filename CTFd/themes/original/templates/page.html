HackCon

About:

HackCon is a one day Capture The Flag contest conducted online where teams from across the world will participate and try to solve questions based on cryptography, reverse engineering, binary exploits, steganography, and web vulnerabilities. Each question will have points assigned to them based on the difficulty level. Grab the most points to win!

Rules:


  1. Attacks on the servers are strictly prohibited
  2. The CTF will follow Jeopardy style format
  3. No physical coercion. This is still a game
  4. Sharing of flags / solutions (online or offline) is strictly against the spirit of the game, and any proof found of sharing will lead to disqualification of the team
  5. Flags are in the format d4rk{[a-zA-Z0-9_]*}c0de unless otherwise mentioned

Event Structure:

The event will consist of one online round where the team that scores the most points will be deemed the winner. In case of a tie, the team that solved the questions worth the tied number of points first will be deemed the winner. There will be 4 awards. 1st place, 2nd place, 3rd place and Best Indian School Team. Awards are only for Indian Teams
